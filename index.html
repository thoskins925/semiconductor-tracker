Before you start — read this once

This is a learning tool. It uses fake money and real-but-delayed daily market data to teach how people read semiconductor stocks.

It talks in terms of probability and risk, never prediction. Nothing here is financial advice, and no indicator tells you what a stock will do next. Markets surprise everyone.

Use it to build intuition and to learn the vocabulary — not to make real trades.
